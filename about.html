RPG Shop

D&D Shopkeeper NPC for Discord & Web

RPG Shop is a free Dungeons & Dragons 5e shopkeeper NPC for Discord, web, and WhatsApp. Browse and buy weapons, armour, potions, adventuring gear, tools, and magic items — all from the same intelligent NPC who remembers your party, tracks your gold, and reacts to your reputation.

Available Items

The full D&D 5e SRD item catalogue, plus homebrew items added by your Dungeon Master:

  • Weapons — Simple Melee, Martial Melee, Simple Ranged, Martial Ranged, by property (Finesse, Heavy, Versatile…), and by weapon type
  • Armour — Light, Medium, Heavy, Shields
  • Adventuring Gear — Potions of Healing, Standard Gear, Kits & Packs
  • Tools — Artisan's Tools, Gaming Sets, Musical Instruments
  • Treasure — Gemstones, Art Objects, Trade Goods
  • Mounts & Vehicles
  • Magic Items — Uncommon through Legendary, custom homebrew, +1/+2/+3 variants

Features

  • Discord bot — use the shop directly in your D&D Discord server
  • Party gold tracker — shared balance and transaction ledger for the whole group
  • Haggling & bartering — negotiate prices with Charisma checks
  • D&D Beyond integration — link your character sheet for automatic stat bonuses
  • DM controls — add homebrew items, set spending limits, hide unavailable items, apply discounts
  • Party stash — shared item storage separate from the shop inventory
  • Personal notes — per-character notes accessible across platforms
  • Weather & mood system — dynamic NPC personality affecting prices and responses
  • Reputation system — earn better prices through repeat business
  • Character switching — manage multiple characters and parties

Platforms

RPG Shop works as a Discord D&D bot, a web chat at rpg-shop.uk, and via WhatsApp. All platforms share the same party inventory and gold balance — buy on Discord, check your balance on web, sell on WhatsApp.

Ideal for Dungeons & Dragons 5e (D&D 5e), Pathfinder, and any tabletop RPG (TTRPG) group that wants an NPC shopkeeper without the spreadsheet.
